🔔 4WARD UPDATE — please do this when you get a sec

The site is now a proper app (real icon, full screen, no browser bar) and it
can send notifications. Takes 2 minutes.

⚠️ FIRST — heads up
If you already have 4WARD on your home screen, you need to DELETE it and add
it again. This will log you out, so make sure you know your password before
you start. If you've forgotten it, message Zeyan first.

📱 IPHONE
1. Delete the old 4WARD icon if you have one (hold it, Remove App)
2. Open 4WARD in Safari — it must be Safari, not Chrome
3. Tap the Share button (square with the arrow pointing up)
4. Scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen", then Add
5. Open 4WARD from the new icon on your home screen
6. Log back in

Important: on iPhone, notifications ONLY work if you open it from the home
screen icon. They will not work in a Safari tab.

🤖 ANDROID
1. Open 4WARD in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots (top right)
3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen", then confirm
4. Open 4WARD from the new icon
5. Log back in if it asks

(On Android notifications also work in a normal browser tab, but installing
gets you the proper icon and full screen, so just do it.)

🔔 THEN TURN ON NOTIFICATIONS (everyone)
1. In 4WARD, tap "More" at the bottom right, then Settings
2. Scroll to Notifications
3. Tap "Turn on notifications"
4. Tap Allow when your phone asks
5. Tap "Send test" — you should get a notification straight away

If the test one arrives, you're done ✅

You'll get notified when:
• A suggestion needs your vote
• Your stats change after the crew approves something
• You rank up a tier
• Someone posts new evidence

This is per device, so if you use it on a laptop too, turn it on there
separately.

❓IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG

"It says add to home screen first"
You're in a Safari tab, not the app. Open it from the home screen icon.

Nothing happens when you tap Send test
Check your phone hasn't blocked them: iPhone → Settings → Notifications →
4WARD → Allow Notifications. Android → Settings → Apps → 4WARD →
Notifications.

Notifications stop arriving later
Just go back into 4WARD Settings and turn them on again. This happens
occasionally, especially if you don't open the app for a while — it's normal,
not broken.

The icon still looks like an old screenshot
You added it before the update. Delete it and add it again.
